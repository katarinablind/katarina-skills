Notes
The minimap is the headline element — large, central, with full frosted-glass chrome. Move the player with WASD or arrows; Q/E (or the mouse) rotate heading. Toggle FOLLOW ↔ ABSOLUTE to feel the two transform offsets. The faint preview behind the minimap shows the implicit world the HUD observes; everything else (frustum cone, POIs, chevron) sits inside the canvas in strict z-order.
