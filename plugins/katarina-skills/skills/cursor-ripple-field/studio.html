Notes
The offset is a pure function of (x, y, t). Each ripple contributes −cos(phase) × ringEnvelope × lifeFade × amp, where ringEnvelope = exp(−(d − age·speed)² / decay²). The ring envelope localizes the wave to the leading edge — that's why it reads as a moving ring instead of a global wobble.
