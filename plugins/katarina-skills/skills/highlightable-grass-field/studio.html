Notes
Grass is one InstancedMesh of PlaneGeometry quads. All sway and proximity coloring happen in a custom ShaderMaterial — the only per-frame JS work is writing two uniforms: uTime and uCursor. The cursor world position comes from raycasting against an invisible ground plane at y=0. Additive blending gives the ghostly halo.
