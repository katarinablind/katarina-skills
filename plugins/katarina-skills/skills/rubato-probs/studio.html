How This Works
Skip
Dark, high-contrast images raise the chance a note is silenced — tension through absence.
Harmony
Contrasty or bright scenes invite harmonic ornamentation — a 3rd voiced above the melody note.
Echo
High contrast drives echo probability — sharp edges in the image translate to rhythmic repetition.
Ornament
Wide saturation spread and brightness lift ornament probability — colorful images produce more grace notes.
